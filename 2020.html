What I have learned in 2020


it’s such a challenging year for me. I definitely have grown and be more mature in 2020.
I was preparing for all the visa application, and all the tax refund then suddenly,I got fired and lost my selected visa lottery because of the pandemic. then I have to find a job within a month. Luckily I got an offer from my current company. I’m pretty enjoying and feeling grateful for what I have now.
1. you will never know what would happen next so always be prepared: always prepare for the lost, always prepare for disappointment, always prepare for the challenge.
2. fight for something that you deserve fight for something that you should earned. if you didn’t speak out loud, they wouldn’t know people wouldn’t help you. Also be vulnerable
3. I have overcome difficult situations in the past so if next time I have an unexpected crisis, always remember everything will be fine in the end. focusing on the very next step to resolve it. Even it's a small step.
4. It’s OK to feel alone sometimes but remember you have your family, your friends and love ones to support you. And yourself.
5. It's OK to be jealous but also look back to what I already have in my life. You may not be happier if you were in their positions. Don't think about the past because it will always make you upset.
6. Money is not the most important thing but it can be a pressure sometimes sooooo having emergency savings is essential.
7. Don't be too proud.
